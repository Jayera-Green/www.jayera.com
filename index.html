Why JAYERA

Smart. Scalable. Sustainable.

Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with deep agricultural expertise to create real impact.

AI-Powered Monitoring

Satellite imagery + drone surveys + computer vision automatically count trees, measure growth, and detect changes—no manual surveys needed.

Verified Carbon Accounting

IPCC-compliant allometric equations calculate carbon sequestration with ±8% uncertainty—trusted by registries worldwide.

Farmers First Mobile App

Offline-capable app in 15+ languages lets farmers report activities, receive payments, and access training—no smartphone required.

Direct Payment System

Smart contracts ensure 85%+ of carbon revenue reaches farmers directly via mobile money, bank transfer, or local cooperatives.

Transparent & Auditable

Every data point is timestamped, geotagged, and stored on immutable ledgers—full traceability for buyers and regulators.

Biodiversity Co-Benefits

Our agroforestry models enhance soil health, water retention, and habitat—creating resilience beyond carbon.

Simple Process

From Farm to Carbon Credit in 4 Steps

We handle the complexity so farmers can focus on what they do best: growing sustainably.

1

Register & Map

Farmers enroll via app or local partner. GPS boundaries are mapped and baseline data collected.

2

AI Monitoring

Satellite + drone imagery analyzes tree growth monthly. Farmers verify via simple photo uploads.

3

Verification & Issuance

Third-party validators review data. Verified credits are issued on blockchain registries.

4

Get Paid

Carbon credits sold to corporations. Revenue distributed directly to farmers within 72 hours.
